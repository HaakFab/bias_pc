Details:

'body': Text of the comment

'group': The social group the comment refers to/was scraped for

'allsides_name': The source name as listed on the Allsides website (see https://www.allsides.com/)

'bias': The Allsides bias for the article’s source (see https://www.allsides.com/media-bias)

'usVSthem_scale': The UsVsThem scale. Closer to 0 means Supportive, closer to 1 is Discriminatory. Neutral is at 1/3 and Critical at 2/3.

'Anger': Whether the comment was annotated as containing Anger towards the social group (other emotions were skipped since it is identical)

'is_Disc_Crit': Binary UsVsThem annotation. True means Critical/Discriminatory, False means Neutral/Supportive.

Publication:

We provide our novel UsVsThem dataset, consisting of nearly 7000 Reddit comments manually annotated via MTurk for populist attitudes, media Bias, basic emotions (6 negative and 6 positive), and social identity. Our Reddit dataset is made publicly available for the research community to be used. Our NLP research was presented and published at the EACL 2021 conference. As a common courtesy the citation of our paper is very appreciated (CC BY-NC 4.0 licence applies):
